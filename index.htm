Gir Cow

The Gir cow is a highly valued dairy breed originating from India, Renowned for its high milk yield and exceptional milk quality, rich in A2 beta-casein protein, it's a popular choice for dairy farms. These cows are also known for their hardiness, adaptability to hot climates, and resistance to diseases. Their docile temperament makes them easy to manage, further contributing to their suitability for dairy farming. The Gir breed's distinctive physical features, including a domed forehead and long, pendulous ears, make them easily recognizable.
